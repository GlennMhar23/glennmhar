Safary Gallery

The owner Mr Chavit Singson has a hunting hobby which becomes his agenda to set up the Baluarte. When his hunting collection trophy grew big he then decided to put up the Baluarte Zoo. This is on top of the hill inside the Baluarte. The Safari Gallery is a "trophy room" of Chavit Singson's collection of animals he hunted for years and years. It features the remains of real animals. You'll see the mounted head, just skin and the whole animals. Some are really massive that I wonder how did they bring it in there. They all look magnificently preserved. Some of the animals in the gallery are bison, wild beast, bear, elephant, lion and deer. Everything is well labelled and the gallery itself is designed flawlessly. And the good thing about this place is it is free for everyone to view. I was really fascinated when I first saw it. It's unbelievable. Here's what you can see in the gallery.




Baluarte Zoo

The Baluarte Zoo is well maintained and is free admission even for the animal show except if you want to take pictures with the Baluarte's big cat (tiger) and other animals, feeding or riding them. Mayor Chavit Singson, as I heard made it free so that the people of Ilocos can come to the zoo and see the animals without the need to go to Manila. You will see animals you typically see in the zoo. You can feed the kangaroo, ride on the donkey, caressed the tiger or put a reptile on your hand or a snake in your shoulder while it slithers lol. They have an amazing interactive show like the wild boar painting and the Fur Sea Experience where you can feed the seals, watch their fantastic show and feed them. They are well-cared for as I see. They are very tamed and gentle. Just feel bad about the tigers chained up in the cage.

Baluarte is a renowned tourist attraction

Baluarte is a breathtaking landmark that attracts thousands of visitors every year. With its stunning architecture and picturesque views, it has become one of the most popular destinations for tourists.

It is located in the beautiful city of Vigan, Philippines

Baluarte is situated in the captivating city of Vigan, known for its well-preserved Spanish colonial architecture and rich cultural heritage. This UNESCO World Heritage Site provides the perfect backdrop for this magnificent landmark.

Baluarte is home to a wide variety of animals

One of the most fascinating aspects of Baluarte is its mini zoo, which houses an impressive collection of animals from all over the world. Visitors can encounter different species, including exotic birds, mammals, reptiles, and even tigers!

There are exciting animal shows and activities

Baluarte offers various animal shows and activities to entertain visitors. From spectacular bird shows to engaging animal encounters, there is always something happening at Baluarte that will leave you amazed and entertained.

Baluarte has a butterfly garden

Immerse yourself in the magical world of butterflies at the Baluarte Butterfly Garden. This enchanting garden is home to a wide variety of butterfly species and offers a serene and tranquil atmosphere, perfect for nature enthusiasts.

You can enjoy thrilling activities at Baluarte

Baluarte offers adventurous activities for visitors seeking a bit of adrenaline. From zip-lining to horseback riding, there are plenty of thrilling experiences to enjoy while exploring this remarkable landmark.
